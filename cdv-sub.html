Gala & Auction 11.03.12

Tickets

Gala & Tickets

The 8th annual Carnivale du Vin will feature a seated wine pairings dinner showcasing New Orleans hospitality, and a “Top Ten U.S. Charity Wine Auction.” The Krewe du Vin preparing this feast includes some of the top names in food and wine. New Orleans artists will be featured throughout the weekend.

Schedule of Events

Friday, November 11

Sponsor Patron Party

  • 6 P.M. – 8 P.M.

    Chef Emeril toasts Grand Marshal sponsors and Krewe du Vin at a welcome reception to celebrate and thank our loyal supporters and friends.

Introducing Boudin & Beer

  • 8 P.M. – 11 P.M.

    Join Chef Emeril, Chef Mario Batali and Chef Donald Link in celebrating regional chefs, artisan sausages, craft brews and Cajun music.

Saturday, November 12

Gala & Auction – N.O. Convention Center – La Nouvelle Ballroom

  • 6:00 P.M. Bacchus Reception

    Join Chef Emeril in toasting with a special bottling by Event Bacchus Dan Kosta of Kosta Browne Winery.

  • 7:00 P.M. Krewe du Vin Dinner

    Exquisite wine pairings dinner with courses by some of the country’s top chefs and vintners.

  • 8:30 P.M. Live Auction

    Our wine auction is filled with one-of-a-kind lifestyle and culinary experiences. Highly exclusive travel and wine packages, including special commemorative lots, guarantee to tempt the most cultivated palates and the most seasoned travelers.

  • 10:00 P.M. Musical Entertainment
